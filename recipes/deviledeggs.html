Deviled Eggs

Description

This recipe is good for using surplus eggs or using as a side dish for a party.

Very minimal number of ingredients makes this a great recipe for people on a budget or are just learning to prepare dishes.

Ingredients

  • 6 large hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard
  • 1 teaspoon pickle juice
